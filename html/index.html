Snowstorm is designed with concept of channel. A channel id is used to identify video streams. Basic actions on channels are:

  • publish a channel when one sends media stream to Snowstorm server.
  • play a channel when one views remote media stream

The following demos show these two basic actions.

Steps to broadcast your webcam:

  1. Click on "Publish my webcam" to obtain a channel id.
  2. Enter id in one of the below video box, click on "Play".
  3. Verify that a video stream is mirror of the webcam.
  4. Try to repeat step 2 and 3 in other boxes or browser tabs.
